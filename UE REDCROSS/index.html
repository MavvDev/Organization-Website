ABOUT US

Established in 2015, the UE Manila Red Cross Youth Council, a volunteer, humanitarian organization believes in the core values, mission and vision of the Philippine Red Cross.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to educate and empower the children and youth in the spirit of Red Cross through constructive trainings and effective leadership, and provide opportunities for directing and harnessing their energy and idealism into worthwhile humanitarian activities.

OUR AIM

The RED CROSS YOUTH COUNCIL OF UNIVERSITY OF THE EAST-MANILA CAMPUS aims to develop and enhance leadership skills of its constituents and members. It further envisions to promote local, national and international friendship.

OUR OBJECTIVE

The organization's objectives encompass advocating humanitarian values, ensuring community health, fostering solidarity, promoting friendship and peace education, and disseminating the Red Cross's principles and International Humanitarian Law.
